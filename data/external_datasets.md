# Recommended Datasets for MUSE (SEO & Content)

- **Amazon Product Reviews**: Massive scale data for product description and sentiment. [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/datasets/amazon_reviews_multi)
- **BestBuy Search Queries**: Understanding user search intent. [iMerit](https://imerit.net/blog/20-best-ecommerce-datasets-for-machine-learning-all-pdp-data-datasets/)
- **Product Details Page (PDP) Data**: Raw product attributes for content synthesis.
